BTS: BangtanBoys

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. They debuted on June 12, 2013 with the song "No More Dream" from their first album 2 Cool 4 Skool. Most popular songs are: "Blood Sweat & Tears" , "MIC DROP", "DNA", "DOPE", "Fire" and others.

EXO

Exo (Korean: 엑소; stylized as EXO) is a South Korean-Chinese boy group based in Seoul. Formed by S.M. Entertainment in 2011, the group debuted in 2012 and has nine members: Suho, Baekhyun, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai, Sehun, Xiumin, Lay and Chen.Their wide variety of musical styles, which have been praised for their diversity, include traditional genres such as pop, hip hop and R&B along with EDM genres including house, trap and synth-pop among others. Most popular songs are: "Ko Ko Bop", "Growl", "Monster", "Power", "The Eve", "Lotto" and others.

RED VELVET

Red Velvet (Hangul: 레드벨벳) is a South Korean girl group formed by S.M. Entertainment. The group debuted on August 1, 2014, with their digital single "Happiness" and with four members: Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy. In March 2015, Red Velvet added a fifth member, Yeri, to the group. Most popular songs are: "Bad Boy", "Peek-A-Boo", "Red Flavor", "Dumb Dumb", "Russian Roulette" and others.

SHINee

Shinee (Korean: 샤이니; stylized as SHINee) is a South Korean boy group formed by S.M. Entertainment in 2008. The group is composed of four members: Onew, Key, Minho, and Taemin. Originally a five-piece group, vocalist Jonghyun died in December 2017. Most popular songs are: "Tell Me What To Do", "Lucifer", "Ring Ding Dong", "1 of 1" and others.

BlackPink

Black Pink (Hangul: 블랙핑크), stylized as BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Most popular songs are: "Boombayah", "Playing With Fire", "Whistle" and others.

Close

Monsta X

Monsta X (Hangul: 몬스타엑스; often stylized as MONSTA X) is a South Korean boy group formed by Starship Entertainment through the survival show No.Mercy on Mnet in 2015. The group is composed of seven members: Jooheon, Shownu, Kihyun, Hyungwon, Wonho, Minhyuk, and I.M. Most popular songs are: "DRAMARAMA", "HERO", "Beautiful", "Shine Forever", "Spotlight" and others.
